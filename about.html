About Tampa Concrete Pros

About Tampa Concrete Pros

Tampa Concrete Pros began with a simple, yet profound vision right here in the heart of Tampa, FL. Founded over a decade ago by local resident Michael "Mike" Thompson, a craftsman with a deep-rooted passion for concrete and an even deeper love for his community, our company was born out of a desire to bring unparalleled quality and integrity to the local concrete industry. Mike saw a consistent need for reliable, expertly installed concrete driveways, patios, and pool decks that could withstand Florida’s unique climate while enhancing the beauty and value of Tampa homes. From those humble beginnings, working out of a small garage in Seminole Heights, we’ve grown steadily, building our reputation one exceptional project at a time. Our ties to Tampa run deep; we’re not just a business operating here, we’re an integral part of the fabric of this vibrant city, committed to supporting its growth and beautifying its neighborhoods.

Our Mission

Our unwavering mission at Tampa Concrete Pros is to serve the homeowners of Tampa, FL, by delivering outstanding concrete driveways, patios, and pool decks that blend superior durability with exquisite craftsmanship. We strive to be the most trusted and respected concrete contractor in the region, known for our integrity, our meticulous attention to detail, and our dedication to complete customer satisfaction. We aim to transform outdoor spaces into beautiful, functional, and lasting additions that elevate the enjoyment and value of every property, consistently exceeding expectations with every slab we pour.

  • Masterful Craftsmanship & Attention to Detail: Our team consists of highly skilled and experienced concrete artisans who approach every project with a meticulous eye for detail and a passion for perfection. We believe that exceptional concrete work goes beyond just pouring; it involves precise grading, expert finishing techniques, and careful curing. This commitment to masterful craftsmanship ensures not only structural soundness but also an aesthetically pleasing finish that significantly enhances your property's curb appeal.

Our Concrete Driveways, Patios, And Pool Decks Expertise

Our team at Tampa Concrete Pros boasts extensive technical expertise and years of hands-on experience in the specialized installation of concrete driveways, patios, and pool decks. We are thoroughly trained in the latest industry best practices, continuously updating our knowledge and techniques to ensure superior results. Our certifications cover advanced concrete finishing, proper sub-base preparation, precise formwork, and the strategic application of reinforcement like rebar and wire mesh—all critical for longevity in Tampa’s unique environment. We understand the nuances of various concrete mixes, curing compounds, and sealing agents, selecting the optimal combination for each specific application to maximize durability and resistance to Florida’s humidity and heat. From intricate stamped concrete designs to perfectly level pool surrounds, our specialized knowledge guarantees a flawless finish and lasting structural integrity, tailor-made for your property.
