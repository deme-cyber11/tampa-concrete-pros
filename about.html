About Tampa Concrete Pros

Serving the Tampa Bay area with quality concrete work since 2012. Licensed, insured, and committed to excellence.

Our Story

Tampa Concrete Pros was founded in 2012 with a simple mission: to provide Tampa Bay homeowners and businesses with exceptional concrete work at fair prices. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the most trusted concrete contractors in the region.

Our founder, Marco Delgado, grew up working alongside his father's masonry crew in Hillsborough County. After earning his contractor's license (Licensed & Insured) and spending 15 years managing large-scale commercial projects, Marco saw an opportunity to bring that same precision and professionalism to residential homeowners. Today, that commitment to excellence drives everything we do.

Over the past decade, we've completed over 2,500 projects across the Tampa Bay area — from simple driveway repairs to large-scale commercial installations. Each project, regardless of size, receives the same attention to detail and dedication to quality that has made us the contractor of choice for discerning property owners.
