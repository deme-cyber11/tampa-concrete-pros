10-Year Concrete Warranty

We stand behind our work with a 10-year workmanship warranty on every concrete installation.

10-Year Workmanship Warranty

All concrete installations by Tampa Concrete Pros are covered for 10 years against workmanship defects.

What's Covered

  • Structural cracking caused by improper installation or sub-base preparation
  • Surface finish defects that appear within the warranty period
  • Settlement or heaving due to inadequate compaction by our crew
  • Stamped concrete pattern defects caused by improper tooling or technique

What's Not Covered

Normal maintenance (sealing), damage from vehicle fluids, cracking from tree root growth not present at time of installation, damage from extreme weather events, or modifications made by others after our installation.

How to Make a Warranty Claim

Contact us at (813) 705-9021 with your project details and a description of the issue. We'll inspect within 5 business days and, if covered, repair or replace at no charge.
