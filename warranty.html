10-Year Warranty

Your investment is protected with our industry-leading warranty coverage.

10-Year Written Warranty

Every project completed by Tampa Concrete Pros is backed by our comprehensive 10-year warranty, covering both materials and workmanship. This warranty is fully transferable if you sell your property.

Home > Warranty

What's Covered

Structural Cracks

Cracks that affect the integrity of the concrete structure

Settling & Sinking

Uneven settling due to improper base preparation

Material Defects

Issues arising from defective concrete materials

Workmanship Issues

Problems resulting from installation errors

What's Not Covered

Cosmetic Hairline Cracks

Minor surface cracks that don't affect structure

Normal Weathering

Natural color changes and surface wear over time

Improper Use

Damage from excessive weight or misuse

Acts of Nature

Hurricane, flood, or earthquake damage

How to File a Claim

  1. Contact Us
    Call (813) 705-9021 or email info@tampaconcretepros.com with your warranty concern.
  2. Provide Documentation
    Share photos of the issue and your original project documentation.
  3. Site Inspection
    We'll schedule a visit to assess the issue within 5 business days.
  4. Resolution
    If covered, we'll repair or replace the affected area at no cost to you.

Warranty Transfer

Our warranty is fully transferable to new property owners. If you sell your home, simply notify us in writing with the new owner's information, and the remaining warranty coverage transfers automatically.
