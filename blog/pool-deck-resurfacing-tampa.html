Pool Deck Resurfacing Options in Tampa, FL

Does Your Tampa Pool Deck Need Refreshing?

Tampa's intense sun, pool chemicals, and heavy foot traffic take their toll on pool decks over time. If your pool deck is discolored, cracked, or just dated-looking, resurfacing can transform it without the cost of a full replacement.

Option 1: Cool Deck Coating

Cool Deck is a spray-applied acrylic topping that adheres to existing concrete. It creates a rough, porous texture that stays noticeably cooler than standard concrete — critical for Tampa summers. Most popular around pools where bare feet are common. Cost: $3–$6/sq ft.

Option 2: Stamped Overlay

A thin concrete overlay (micro-topping) applied over existing concrete and then stamped. Gives the look of new stamped concrete without full tear-out. Works when the existing slab is structurally sound with minor surface issues. Cost: $8–$14/sq ft.

Option 3: Acrylic Concrete Sealer

For decks in good structural condition with mainly cosmetic issues, a fresh pressure wash and professional-grade acrylic sealer can restore color and add a fresh look at a fraction of resurfacing cost. Cost: $1–$3/sq ft.

Option 4: Full Replacement

For pool decks with structural cracks, significant settling, or advanced deterioration, full replacement is the most durable solution. New concrete with proper reinforcement and drainage will last another 30 years.

How to Choose

The right choice depends on your deck's current condition and your budget. We offer free inspections to assess your pool deck and recommend the most cost-effective solution. Call (813) 705-9021 to schedule.
