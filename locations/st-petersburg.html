How does the coastal environment in St. Petersburg affect concrete?

St. Petersburg's coastal location in Pinellas County exposes concrete to salt air, higher humidity, and wind-driven moisture that can accelerate surface deterioration and rebar corrosion over time. We address this by using high-strength concrete mixes, corrosion-resistant reinforcement, and marine-grade sealants that protect against salt intrusion. Proper curing and sealing are especially critical near Tampa Bay and the Gulf of Mexico shoreline.

Stamped or decorative concrete ranges from $13 to $27 per square foot depending on pattern complexity and color.
