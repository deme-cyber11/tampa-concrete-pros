Does salt air on Clearwater Beach damage concrete?

Salt air accelerates surface wear and can cause rebar corrosion inside concrete over time, especially on properties near Clearwater Beach and the Intracoastal Waterway. We combat this by using high-strength concrete mixes, epoxy-coated or corrosion-resistant rebar, and marine-grade penetrating sealants that block salt and moisture intrusion. Proper sealing and periodic resealing every 2 to 3 years will keep your Clearwater concrete looking great and structurally sound.
