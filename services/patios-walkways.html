Our Installation Process

From design to completion, we ensure every step meets our high standards.

1

Design Consultation

We visit your property, discuss your vision, measure the space, and help you choose the perfect style and finish.

2

Site Preparation

We excavate, grade for proper drainage, and install a compacted gravel base with necessary reinforcement.

3

Pour & Finish

Fresh concrete is poured, leveled, and finished to your specifications. Stamping or texturing is applied if chosen.

4

Cure & Seal

We apply curing compound and protective sealer. After proper cure time, your space is ready to enjoy.
