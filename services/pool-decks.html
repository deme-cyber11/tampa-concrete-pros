Pool Deck Finish Options

A concrete pool deck is the paved surface area surrounding a swimming pool, designed to provide safe, slip-resistant, and comfortable walking surfaces for swimmers. Pool decks must withstand constant water exposure, pool chemicals, UV radiation, and heavy foot traffic while remaining cool enough for barefoot use.

Florida Pool Statistics: Florida has more residential swimming pools than any other state, with over 1.7 million pools statewide. The Tampa Bay area alone has the highest concentration of pools per capita in the nation, making pool deck installation and resurfacing a critical service for homeowners in our region.

Source: Pool & Hot Tub Alliance, Florida Market Report 2024

Benefits of Professional Pool Decks

A properly designed pool deck enhances safety, comfort, and the overall enjoyment of your pool.

Pool Safety Impact: According to the Consumer Product Safety Commission, slip-resistant pool deck surfaces can reduce poolside slip-and-fall accidents by up to 50%. Proper drainage design further reduces standing water hazards that contribute to approximately 6,000 pool-area injuries annually in the United States.

Source: U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission, Pool Safety Data

Safety Features for Florida Pools

We incorporate multiple safety features into every pool deck installation.

Heat Safety Data: Standard concrete pool decks in Tampa can reach surface temperatures of 140-150 degrees F during summer afternoons. Cool deck coatings reduce this to approximately 100-110 degrees F, making barefoot walking safe and comfortable. This 30-40 degree temperature reduction is essential for Florida pool owners.

Source: University of Florida IFAS Extension, Building Materials Study

Slip-Resistant Textures

All our finishes include textured surfaces that provide traction even when wet, reducing slip and fall risks around your pool.

Heat-Reflective Technology

Cool deck options reflect solar heat, keeping surface temperatures 30-40 degrees cooler than standard concrete to prevent burns and discomfort.

Proper Drainage Slopes

We engineer slight slopes away from the pool to prevent standing water, which creates both safety hazards and maintenance issues.

Smooth Edges & Transitions

We eliminate sharp edges and create smooth transitions to the pool coping, reducing scrapes and injuries.
