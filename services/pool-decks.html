Safety Features for Florida Pools

We incorporate multiple safety features into every pool deck installation.

Slip-Resistant Textures

All our finishes include textured surfaces that provide traction even when wet, reducing slip and fall risks around your pool.

Heat-Reflective Technology

Cool deck options reflect solar heat, keeping surface temperatures 30% cooler than standard concrete to prevent burns.

Proper Drainage Slopes

We engineer slight slopes away from the pool to prevent standing water, which creates both safety hazards and maintenance issues.

Smooth Edges & Transitions

We eliminate sharp edges and create smooth transitions to the pool coping, reducing scrapes and injuries.
